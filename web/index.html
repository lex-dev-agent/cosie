Questions & answers

Is it safe for the crib?

Cosie is designed with nursery safety in mind. No hard edges, no small detachable parts, and made from baby-safe, non-toxic materials. It sits alongside your baby, not on them — no wearables, no clips, no socks.

How does it connect without Wi-Fi?

Cosie uses low-power cellular connectivity (similar to how a GPS tracker works). It sends tiny packets of temperature data over the mobile network. This means it works everywhere with cell coverage — at home, at the grandparents', at the bach, even in the car.

Does it measure my baby's body temperature?

No — and intentionally so. Cosie monitors the ambient temperature of your baby's sleep environment (the air around them in the crib). This is what sleep safety guidelines from Plunket and the AAP focus on. It's not a medical device.

How long does the battery really last?

12 months of continuous monitoring on a single charge. The electronics module uses an ultra-low-power cellular chip designed for IoT devices that send small amounts of data infrequently — perfect for temperature readings every few minutes.

What temperature range does it alert on?

You set your preferred range in the app. Sleep safety guidelines recommend 16–20°C (61–68°F) for babies. If the temperature around your baby drifts above or below your set range, you'll get a push notification on your phone.

Is there a monthly subscription?

No. 12 months of cellular connectivity, cloud service, and full app access are included in the purchase price. After 12 months, you can optionally renew for $4.99/month if you want to keep monitoring.

Can I wash it?

The electronics module is removable. Take it out, and the teddy is fully machine washable. Pop the module back in when it's dry.
