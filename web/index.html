Questions

Is it safe?

Cosie is designed for babies. Soft materials, no hard edges, no small parts, no wearables. It sits beside your little one, not on them.

How does it connect?

Cosie has its own built-in connection, like a GPS tracker. No setup, no passwords, no range limits. If there's mobile coverage, Cosie is connected.

What does it actually monitor?

The temperature of the air around your baby. Their environment, not their body. Health guidelines recommend keeping it between 16–22°C. It's not a medical device. It's a comfort product that gives you useful information.

Does the battery really last 12 months?

Yes. The electronics inside are designed to sip power, sending tiny updates every few minutes, it uses almost nothing. One charge covers the full first year.

Are there ongoing costs?

No. The first 12 months of everything (connectivity, cloud, app) are included in the price. That covers the full SIDS risk window. After that, you can optionally keep going for $49/year or $4.99/month.

Can I wash it?

The electronics pop out. The teddy goes in the washing machine. Pop the module back in when it's dry.
